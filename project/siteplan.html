Website Planning Document

Site Name: Bright English Speakers Tutorial Center

Description: "brightenglishspeakers.com". I chose it because it's professional, playful, and has great potential.

Site Purpose: English as a Second Language (ESL) Tutorial

Scope: Promotes to study English from English Tutors;
Services: ESL one on one lessons and shows word of the day for website visitors;
Information: Shows teacher's contact information. Some testimonials and promos.

Scenarios or questions from site visitor's point of view:

What will be the benefits of enrolling to an English Tutor?

How to enroll?

How long does a class usually last?

How much do classes cost?

What are the teacher's credentials

How can I pay?
